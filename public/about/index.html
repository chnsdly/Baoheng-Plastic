How We Build and Deliver

Factory floor readiness, QA labs, and container loading to keep projects on schedule.

Factory floor readiness

20,000 m² workshop with 400–2800T injection and 1,000L blow-molding lines to cover pallets, bins, and cans.

  • Dedicated lanes for pallets, crates, and sanitation SKUs
  • Tooling team onsite to adjust ribs, thickness, and fit
  • Color and logo changeovers handled on the same shift

Quality and material testing

Incoming resin checks, IPQC sampling, and simulated load tests keep every batch reliable.

  • Impact, stacking, cold-room, and forklift trials
  • Material traceability with production date and source
  • Food-grade and anti-static options validated per run

Container loading & logistics

Packing plans optimized for consolidation, nested designs, and safe arrival at ports and warehouses.

  • FOB/CIF/DDP experience with full-container and LCL
  • Protective wrapping and palletization by SKU
  • Shipment photos and loading reports for every release

Manufacturing Capabilities

Tooling · Molding · Materials · Quality · Fulfillment

Processes

  • Injection molding (400–2800T) for pallets, bins, and trash cans
  • Blow molding (up to 1,000L) for lightweight pallets and tanks
  • Rotomolding for large parts and low-volume specials

Materials

  • HDPE / PP options with food-grade and anti-static choices
  • HDPE+POE impact modification for high-turnover scenarios
  • Up to 30% recycled content balancing strength and cost

Quality

  • IQC for incoming materials · IPQC in process · OQC before shipment
  • Stacking, drop, forklift, and cold-resistance simulation tests
  • Batch traceability with production date and material source

Tooling & Sampling

  • In-house tooling shop to tune ribs and thickness by pallet/bin
  • Rapid sampling in 7–10 days
  • Custom colors, logos, label zones, and barcode areas

Fulfillment

  • EXW/FOB/CIF/DDP with experience in full-container, consolidation, and bulk packaging
  • Nestable designs and loading optimization to cut logistics cost
  • Multilingual support with stacking and usage guidance

Project Management

  • Scenario-based proposals for cold chain, warehousing, and sanitation
  • Transparent schedules with remote inspection at key milestones
  • After-sales support with replenishment and tooling upkeep
