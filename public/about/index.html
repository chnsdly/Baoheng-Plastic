What you can expect from Baoheng

The strongest projects start with clear application details and end with products that match the site, not just the catalog.

Selection support before price comparison

Before quoting, we clarify your application, load, rack, forklift, temperature, hygiene, and return-flow details. This makes different options easier to compare.

  • Product family and model direction based on real use
  • Specification review for load, material, and structure
  • RFQ details organized for internal review

Factory control for repeatable orders

Manufacturing, tooling adjustment, material selection, and inspection are coordinated around each product family so repeat orders stay consistent.

  • HDPE/PP material options and color management
  • Standard models plus practical customization
  • Batch inspection before shipment release

Packing and export communication for bulky plastic products

Plastic pallets and containers need careful packing and container loading plans. We support SKU separation, loading photos, and shipping coordination.

  • Container loading quantity and packing review
  • Shipment and loading photos when needed
  • FOB/CIF and repeat replenishment communication

Where Baoheng Fits Best

Development Path

  2. 2016

  3. 2019
