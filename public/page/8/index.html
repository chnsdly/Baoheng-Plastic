Factory capability

Manufacturing capability behind the quotation

Baoheng Plastic combines molding equipment, material selection, tooling adjustment, production inspection, and export packing to help you compare specifications, risk, and cost together.

View manufacturing capability

Material and structure selection

HDPE/PP options, impact-modified formulas, recycled-content choices, anti-slip surfaces, steel reinforcement, and color/logo requirements are selected around the operating scenario.

Molding and tooling support

Injection and blow-molding production supports standard models and project adjustments such as ribs, deck texture, label zones, and reinforced structures.

Inspection, packing, and export delivery

Batch checks, visual inspection, stacking tests, packing plans, shipment photos, and container loading support help reduce arrival and use-risk after delivery.
