Factory capability

Plastic Pallet Manufacturer

We focus on plastic pallet R&D and manufacturing, serving warehousing, logistics, packaging, cold chain, food, chemical, and agricultural industries. Made from virgin HDPE/PP and formed by advanced one-piece injection molding, our pallets feature a well-engineered structure, high load capacity, and a longer service life. With 15 large injection molding machines, we offer a full range of standard sizes and load ratings for common applications. We also provide strong tooling and mold development capabilities, supporting customization in size, color, logo, and steel-tube reinforcement. Our experienced team offers one-on-one support from model selection through mass delivery. Compatible with forklifts, racking, and automated warehouses, Baoheng pallets help improve handling efficiency and reduce total logistics costs.

Reliable Material System

Made with premium HDPE/PP virgin resins and functional modifiers, providing high toughness, impact resistance, low-temperature durability, and chemical resistance as the foundation for pallet performance.

Large-Scale Manufacturing

Multiple large injection molding lines running 24/7, supported by automated auxiliary equipment, cooling systems, and mold-temperature control. Each batch follows process inspections and barcode traceability for consistent quality.

Finished Products & Inventory

Full range of pallets including flat-top, three-runner, full-perimeter, double-faced, cold-chain, and chemical pallets. Strong inventory capability and customization options such as colors, logos, and steel-reinforced structures.
