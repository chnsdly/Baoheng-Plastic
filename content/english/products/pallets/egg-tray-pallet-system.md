detail_intro: |-
  1. This solution follows a modular “base pallet + divider + egg tray” stacking structure. The plastic pallet supports forklift handling, divider sheets distribute interlayer loads, and egg trays position individual eggs. It is suitable for egg collection, sorting hubs, and pre-dispatch distribution workflows.

  2. Layer quantity can be configured by transport requirement. Under typical setups, 4-layer, 5-layer, and 6-layer systems correspond to approximately 8,640 / 10,800 / 12,960 eggs per set, with about 288 / 360 / 432 trays respectively. These figures are reference values and should be validated against tray specification, handling method, and transport conditions.

  3. Typical system envelope dimensions may be referenced as approximately 1.2 × 0.9 × 1.5 m, 1.2 × 0.9 × 1.82 m, and 1.2 × 0.9 × 2.15 m. We recommend checking door clearance, vehicle internal height, forklift mast limits, and stacking policy before deployment.

  4. The system is designed for standardized returnable handling and higher bulk movement efficiency, making it suitable for egg logistics operations seeking lower breakage risk and more consistent loading/unloading practices. We can provide matching recommendations based on your pallet footprint, tray format, and turnover frequency.
